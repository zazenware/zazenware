Our commitment

zazenware aims to meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, across every page.

How we work toward this

  • Semantic HTML landmarks (header, nav, main, footer).
  • Visible focus on every interactive control.
  • Keyboard-operable navigation, forms, cart, and theme toggle.
  • Text alternatives for images that convey meaning.
  • Colour contrast that meets AA on every theme combination we ship.
  • Honest labelling — colour is never the only indicator of state.
  • Respect for prefers-reduced-motion.

Known limitations

To be populated honestly as issues are discovered during launch QA (epic E-14).

Tell us if something doesn't work

Email contact@zazenware.com with the subject line "Accessibility" and describe what you ran into. Include your browser and device if you can. We aim to acknowledge within 7 days.
