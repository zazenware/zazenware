Plain summary

All zazenware goods are made to order. Returns are case-by-case. Defective items and shipping mistakes are always made right at our expense. We try to be fair and human about everything else.

What's always covered

We will replace or refund at our expense, with no questions:

  • Goods that arrive damaged due to shipping.
  • Goods that arrive defective (printing errors, broken stitching, ink issues that fall outside expected variation).
  • Goods sent in the wrong size, colour, or design.

What's normally not refundable

Because each item is made to order specifically for you:

  • Change-of-mind / buyer's remorse returns are not normally accepted.
  • Sizing mistakes (wrong size ordered) are the customer's responsibility — please check the size chart before ordering.
  • "It's not exactly like the photo" is not a defect if the variation is within the expected range for hand-printed goods (minor colour shifts, slight ink placement variation, fine texture differences).

That said, we're a small artist-run shop. If you have an unusual circumstance, please email us. We will review each case and make a fair decision.

How to start a return or refund

Email contact@zazenware.com with:

  1. Your order number.
  2. A description of the issue.
  3. Clear photos of the item and packaging (especially helpful for damage claims).

We aim to respond within 3 business days.

Timeframes

  • Damaged or defective items — please contact us within 7 days of delivery.
  • Wrong item shipped — please contact us within 14 days of delivery.
  • Other inquiries — anytime; we'll review what's possible.

Return shipping cost

If the return is for our error or a defect, we cover return shipping. We'll either send you a prepaid return label or refund the shipping cost separately once we see your receipt.

In the rare cases where we accept a customer-initiated return, the customer pays the return shipping.

How refunds are issued

Refunds are sent via Canadian Interac e-transfer to the email used for the original order. Refunds are typically issued within 5 business days of approval.

We do not refund to credit cards because we don't process credit card payments — all payments are by e-transfer.

Partial refunds

Where appropriate (for example, a multi-item order where one item is defective), we will refund just the affected portion, including its proportional share of taxes. Shipping is refunded only when the entire order is refunded.

Cancellation before payment

If you have submitted an order but not yet sent your e-transfer, you can cancel free of charge — just email us with the order number. We will mark the order as cancelled and ignore any future e-transfer attempts for it.

Cancellation after payment

Once we've received your e-transfer:

  • If we haven't started production — we can cancel and refund in full.
  • If production has started — we may refund partially or in full at our discretion, depending on materials used and labour invested.
  • If the order has shipped — it's no longer cancellable; see "Returns" above.

Questions

Any return or refund question: contact@zazenware.com with your order number.
