Plain summary

All zazenware goods are made-to-order. Returns are case-by-case. Defective items and shipping mistakes are always made right at our expense.

Damaged in transit

Email contact@zazenware.com within 7 days of delivery with your order number and photos. We'll replace the item or refund it via e-transfer.

Wrong item shipped

We cover the cost of return shipping and ship the correct item at no charge to you.

Customer-initiated returns

Because goods are made-to-order, we generally don't accept returns for buyer's remorse. For special circumstances, message us — the artist reviews each case individually.

How refunds are issued

Via Canadian Interac e-transfer to the email used for the original order. Refunds are typically issued within 5 business days of approval.
