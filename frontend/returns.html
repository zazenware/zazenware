shipping & returns

What to expect when your order arrives, and how returns work.

Shipping

All orders ship within 3–5 business days from the date you submit your order and e-transfer payment. We ship Canada-wide via Canada Post.

Shipping rates: $10 flat rate to all provinces except Ontario, where shipping is free.

Delivery times: Typically 5–10 business days depending on your province. Remote areas may take longer.

You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships. Track your package directly with Canada Post.

Returns & Exchanges

Items can be returned within 14 days of delivery if they arrive damaged or if there's a mistake in your order. We'll replace or refund via e-transfer.

Damaged items: Contact us immediately with photos. We'll send a replacement at no cost.

Wrong item: Our mistake? We'll fix it. Send it back and we'll ship the correct one.

Changed your mind? Items are final sale unless defective. Custom orders cannot be returned.

To start a return, email orders@zazenware.com with your order number and photos if applicable.

Materials & Care

Shirts: 100% cotton, screen-printed. Machine wash cold, hang dry. Print will last with gentle care.

Patches: Embroidered canvas, iron-on backing. Iron on or sew. Washable but best for display or careful use.

Prints: Archival matte paper, pigment-based ink. Frame under glass for best longevity. Keep out of direct sunlight.
