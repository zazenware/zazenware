shipping policy

Where we ship, what it costs, how long it takes, and what happens when something goes wrong.

Effective: 2026-05-10. Last reviewed: 2026-05-10.

Where we ship

zazenware ships to addresses in Canada only at this time. We do not currently ship internationally. We may add international shipping in the future; for now, if you are outside Canada and interested in our work, message contact@zazenware.com and we'll let you know when international shipping becomes available.

Shipping cost

  • Free shipping within Ontario.
  • $10 CAD flat-rate to every other Canadian province and territory.

The shipping cost is the same whether your order has one item or many. It applies per order, not per item.

How we ship

We ship via Canada Post or a comparable Canadian courier, depending on package size and destination. We choose the most reliable affordable option for each order.

Handling time

Goods are made to order. Handling time may take up to approximately 3 weeks from the date your e-transfer is confirmed. This is the time between receiving payment and handing the package to the courier. Shipping (transit) time is in addition to this and depends on the destination — usually a few business days within most of Canada, longer to remote areas and territories.

If you need an order for a specific date (birthday, gift, event), please message us before ordering and we'll let you know what's realistic.

Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information when the courier provides one. Some shipping methods to remote areas may not include tracking.

Order status

You can think of your order's lifecycle in four stages:

  1. Pending payment — you submitted the order; we're waiting for your e-transfer.
  2. Paid — we've matched your e-transfer to your order; production has started.
  3. Fulfilled — your order has shipped.
  4. Cancelled — the order was cancelled (by you or by us).

You'll receive emails at each status change. If you don't hear anything for more than a week after paying, please email contact@zazenware.com with your order number.

Damaged in transit

If your order arrives damaged, please email contact@zazenware.com within 7 days of delivery with your order number and photos of the damage and the packaging. We'll either replace the item or refund it via e-transfer at our discretion.

Lost in transit

If your tracking shows delivery but you didn't receive the package, first check with neighbours and your local post office. If the package can't be located after 7 days, email us and we'll work with the courier to file a claim. Depending on the outcome, we will either replace or refund the order.

Incorrect address

Please double-check your shipping address before submitting your order. If you realize you made a mistake, email us as soon as possible — if we haven't shipped yet, we can correct it. If the package has already shipped and is returned to us undeliverable, you'll be responsible for the cost of re-shipping. If the package is lost because of an incorrect address, we are not able to refund or replace it.

Wrong item shipped

If we send you the wrong item, that's our error. Email us with your order number and a photo of what arrived. We'll cover the cost of return shipping and send the correct item at no charge to you.

Customs and duties

Because we ship within Canada only, there are no customs or duties to worry about.

Questions

Any shipping question: contact@zazenware.com.
