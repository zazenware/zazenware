Where we ship, what it costs, and how long it takes.

Effective: 2026-05-10.

Where we ship

zazenware ships to addresses in Canada only at this time.

Shipping cost

  • Free shipping within Ontario.
  • $10 CAD flat-rate to every other Canadian province and territory.

Handling time

Goods are made-to-order. Handling time may take up to approximately 3 weeks from the date your e-transfer is confirmed. Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking when available.

Damaged in transit

If your order arrives damaged, email contact@zazenware.com within 7 days of delivery with your order number and photos.
