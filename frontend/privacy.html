privacy policy

What we collect, why, how long we keep it, and how to ask for it back.

Effective: 2026-05-10. Last reviewed 2026-05-10.

Plain summary

zazenware collects the minimum personal information needed to fulfill your order or answer your message. We don't track you, sell your data, or send marketing emails.

What we collect

When you place an order: your name, email, optional note, shipping address, the items you ordered, the order total, and timestamps.

When you contact us: your name, email, inquiry type, message.

On every visit: standard server request metadata (IP address, browser type) for security purposes. We do not use analytics or tracking cookies.

In your browser: a localStorage entry for your cart and your light/dark theme choice. This data lives only on your device.

Who we share it with

Resend, the service that delivers your order confirmation email. Nobody else.

Your rights (PIPEDA)

You can request a copy of the data we hold about you, request corrections, or request deletion. Email contact@zazenware.com with the subject line "Privacy request".

Full canonical copy will be installed during epic E-13 (Master Spec § 11.1).
