Your privacy matters

privacy policy

We don't sell data. We don't track you. Simple as that.

Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect: your name, email, shipping address, and phone number (optional). This information is used only to process and ship your order.

When you contact us, we store your message and email to respond to your inquiry.

We do not collect payment information. E-transfers are handled directly by your bank.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used only to:

  • Process and ship your order
  • Send order confirmation and tracking emails
  • Respond to your contact messages
  • Resolve disputes or returns

We do not sell, share, or trade your data with third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in our order database. We use industry-standard encryption and access controls.

We do not store credit card or banking information. E-transfers are handled directly by your financial institution.

Tracking & Cookies

zazenware does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or advertising pixels. We do not track your browsing behavior.

This site is designed to be fast, private, and zero-surveillance.

Your Rights

You can request a copy of the data we hold about you, or ask us to delete it. Email orders@zazenware.com with your request.

You can unsubscribe from order emails by replying "unsubscribe" (though we only send transactional emails, not marketing).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy over time. Material changes will be posted on this page with the date updated.

Questions?

Email us at orders@zazenware.com with any privacy concerns or questions.
