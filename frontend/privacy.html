Legal

privacy policy

What we collect, why, how long we keep it, and how to ask for it back.

Effective: 2026-05-10. Last reviewed: 2026-05-10.

Plain summary

zazenware is a small, artist-run shop based in Ontario, Canada. We collect the minimum personal information needed to fulfill your order or answer your message. We don't track you across the web, sell your data, run advertising, or send marketing emails.

This policy describes what we collect, why we collect it, how long we keep it, who we share it with, and how to exercise your rights under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

Who we are

zazenware is operated by a single artist in Ontario, Canada. For all privacy-related questions, contact contact@zazenware.com.

What we collect

When you place an order

  • Your name (for the order record).
  • Your email address (so we can send the order confirmation and fulfillment update).
  • Your shipping name and Canadian shipping address (so we can deliver the order).
  • Your optional order note (if you choose to include one).
  • The items you ordered, their prices, and the order total.
  • The order timestamps (when you submitted it, when it was paid, when it was fulfilled).
  • The status of your transactional email (sent, failed, skipped) and the time it was sent.

We do not collect or store payment card information. Payment happens outside the website via Canadian Interac e-transfer.

When you message us through the contact form

  • Your name.
  • Your email address.
  • Your selected inquiry type.
  • Your message.
  • For "print my existing design" inquiries, your confirmation that you have rights to the artwork.

On every visit (technical data)

  • Server request metadata — your IP address, browser user-agent string, and the URL you requested. We use this only for security (rate limiting, abuse detection) and for diagnosing site errors. It is not used to build a profile of you.
  • Cookies — we do not use cookies for advertising, analytics, or tracking. Authentication isn't used because we don't have user accounts.

In your browser (localStorage)

The site stores two pieces of data in your browser's localStorage:

  • zw-cart — your shopping cart, so it persists between page loads.
  • zw-theme — your light/dark theme preference.

This data lives only on your device. It is never sent to our servers. You can clear it any time by opening your browser's site settings and clearing local data for this site, or by using the "Clear cart" button on the cart page.

Why we collect each thing

  • Name + email + shipping address — to deliver your order and reach you about it.
  • Order details + totals — to fulfill your order accurately and keep accounting records as required by Canadian tax law.
  • Contact form submissions — to answer your question or process your inquiry.
  • Server request metadata — to keep the site secure and diagnose errors.

How we use it

We use your personal information only to:

  • Send order confirmation and fulfillment emails.
  • Fulfill and ship your physical order.
  • Respond to your inquiries.
  • Maintain accounting records as required by Canadian tax law (currently 6 years per CRA guidance).
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse.

We will not use your information for marketing emails, advertising, profiling, automated decision-making, or selling to third parties.

Who we share it with

We share the minimum data necessary with these third parties:

  • Resend (USA) — receives your email address and the body of the order confirmation email, in order to deliver it to your inbox. Resend privacy policy ↗
  • Railway (USA) — hosts the application and database. Your data is stored on their infrastructure. Railway privacy policy ↗
  • Netlify (USA) — serves the website's static files. Server logs (IP, user-agent) are processed here. Netlify privacy policy ↗
  • Canada Post or equivalent courier — receives your shipping name and address to deliver the physical order.

Because Resend, Railway, and Netlify are based in the United States, your information may be transferred to and stored in the U.S. We chose providers with reputable privacy practices and standard contractual protections.

We do not sell your personal information, ever. We do not share it with advertisers or marketers.

How long we keep it

  • Order records — 6 years from the date of the order (Canadian tax law).
  • Contact form messages — up to 2 years from receipt, after which they're archived or deleted.
  • Server access logs — up to 30 days for security investigations.
  • Browser localStorage — until you clear it yourself; we cannot delete it remotely.

Your rights (PIPEDA)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your information, subject to legal retention requirements (we may need to keep order records for tax purposes).
  • Withdraw consent — for any future processing.
  • Complain — to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at priv.gc.ca.

To exercise any of these rights, email contact@zazenware.com with the subject line "Privacy request". We will acknowledge within 7 days and respond within 30 days.

Security

We use HTTPS across the entire site. Database backups are encrypted. The cart and form data never leave your browser until you submit. Email transmission uses TLS where the receiving server supports it. No system is perfectly secure, but we make reasonable efforts to protect your information.

Children's privacy

zazenware is not directed at children under 14. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 14. If you believe we have, please contact us and we'll delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the "Last reviewed" date at the top of this page. For substantive changes, we'll post a notice on the site. Continued use after a change constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Privacy questions, requests, or complaints: contact@zazenware.com with the subject line "Privacy request".
